Who I am

I'm João Paulo Gonçalves, Design Specialist at YDUQS, one of the largest education companies in Brazil. With over 10 years of experience in Design, UX/UI, and Digital Products, I create interfaces, develop tests, and build product concepts for IBMEC, Estácio, and other brands in the group.

I hold an MBA in Business Management from Ibmec and multiple specializations in user experience, design systems, and digital product management. I bridge interface and user experience, aiming to deliver value to the customer and solve real problems.

I also have a background in branding, marketing, and communications, acquired at advertising agencies. I value teamwork, leadership, and continuous improvement.
