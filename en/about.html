ABOUT

Design Systems specialist and design infrastructure expert with 10+ years building products that scale.

Who I am

I'm João Paulo Gonçalves, Design Specialist at YDUQS. I work at the intersection of product, systems and design operations — building the infrastructure that enables teams to deliver with consistency, speed and quality at scale.

I lead the evolution of Lift 2.0, a multi-brand Design System that unifies IBMEC, Estácio and other brands in the group. My work goes beyond interfaces: it involves component architecture, governance, documentation, tokens and engineering integration.

I hold an MBA in Business Management from Ibmec and complementary training in Design Systems, Product Design and UX Research. My approach combines systems thinking, data-driven decisions and focus on organizational impact.

How I work

Design Infrastructure

I build and evolve complete Design Systems — tokens, components, documentation and governance. Structures that scale without depending on individuals.

Design Operations

I optimize workflows between design and engineering. Reduce handoff, eliminate rework and accelerate delivery cycles with clear processes.

Product & Strategy

I make design decisions grounded in data, research and business metrics. I prioritize measurable impact over aesthetic preference.

AI applied to Design Ops

I explore tools like ChatGPT, Kiro and Figma AI to automate documentation, generate specs and optimize design operations.
